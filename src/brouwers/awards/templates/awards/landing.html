{% blocktrans trimmed %} Currently the awards functionality is disabled because we are re-working it, sorry for that!

Normally, you can nominate topics for every edition - e.g. in 2021 you can nominate projects that were finished between Jan. 1st 2021 and Dec. 31st 2021. Moderators will validate every submission by checking if the topic was indeed finished in that year.

Between Jan. 1st and Jan. 15th of the next year (2022 in the example), users can submit their votes.

Finally, after the voting period, the top-three within each category are recorded in our hall-of-fame. {% endblocktrans %}
